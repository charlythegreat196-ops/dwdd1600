Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most Famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas Jefferson was a Founding Father, the principal author of the Declaration of Independence, and the third president of the United States. A proponent of democracy and agrarianism, he orchestrated the Louisiana Purchase, doubling the nation's size, and founded the University of Virginia.
John Adams
John Adams was a Founding Father and the second president of the United States from 1797 to 1801. Before his presidency, he was a leader of the American Revolution that achieved independence from Great Britain.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the U.S.A. and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declaration, an act considered treasonous by the british crown.
